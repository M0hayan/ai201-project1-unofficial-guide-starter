International Student Housing Information

Illinois State University offers multiple housing options for new international students, including on-campus residence halls, university apartments, privately owned apartments, and temporary housing options.

Students under the age of 17 are not eligible to live in University-operated housing, including residence halls and university-owned apartments unless approved by the Housing Director.

ON-CAMPUS HOUSING OPTIONS

Residence Halls & Cardinal Court Apartments
- First-year international students are generally required to live in university housing for their first two years after high school
- Housing applications must be completed through University Housing Services
- Not all residence halls remain open during break periods (Fall, Winter, Spring)

Break housing availability:
- Hewett & Manchester: 9-month housing (limited break access)
- Wilkins: Open over Fall Break
- Cardinal Court: 11-month housing (open through most breaks)
- Summer housing requires a separate contract and placement in an open facility

Students living in residence halls are required to purchase a meal plan.

International House Themed Living Learning Community (TLLC)
- Specialized floors supporting international students
- Provides cultural programs, trips, and transition support
- Limited space; assignments are first-come, first-served (for students not required to live on campus)
- Meal plan required
- Contact: mlschwa@ilstu.edu

UNIVERSITY-OWNED APARTMENTS
- Includes Fell Avenue and School Street apartments
- Intended primarily for students who have been out of high school for more than 2 years
- Students must contact University Housing Services for placement
- Application through official housing system required

PRIVATELY OWNED APARTMENTS (OFF-CAMPUS HOUSING)
- Managed by private landlords, not the university
- Students are responsible for leases and arrangements

Resources:
- Off-Campus Services (Dean of Students Office)
  - Housing database (ISU login required)
  - Roommate finder tool
  - Lease guidance and support
- Contact: (309) 438-2008 | deanofstudents@ilstu.edu

Important notes:
- Lease agreements are legally binding and cannot easily be terminated once signed
- Students should verify distance from campus, furnishing status, and utility costs before signing
- Networking with current students and Registered Student Organizations (RSOs) can help find housing
- International student groups and Facebook communities may help with roommates

TEMPORARY HOUSING OPTIONS

Hotels near campus:
- Hyatt Place (Uptown Normal)
- Marriott Hotel (Uptown Normal)

Local organizations (not university affiliated):
- International Students Inc. (ISI)
  - Temporary housing, transportation assistance, friendship programs
- International Friends of ISU
  - Host families, arrival assistance, housing support, community events

REGISTERED STUDENT ORGANIZATIONS (RSOs)
- Student organizations may help with housing connections and social integration
- Full directory available through ISU RSO listings

CONTACT
For housing questions, contact International Student and Scholar Services or visit official ISU housing resources.
