SECTION: Summer Housing
Summer Housing is available at Illinois State University with flexible options including weekly contracts or full-summer stays. Enrollment in summer classes is not required. This housing is designed for interns, conference guests, researchers, and students needing short-term or extended stays. Applications are submitted through the Housing and Dining Portal.

For questions, contact Housing Services at (309) 438-8611.

SECTION: Important Dates - Move-Out 2026
All residence halls and on-campus apartments close for summer on May 9, 2026 at 2:00 p.m.

SECTION: Important Dates - Move-In 2026
Move-in occurs from August 11 to August 15, 2026.

SECTION: Fall Break Housing
Watterson, Wright, and Haynie close on November 21 at 2:00 p.m. and reopen November 29 at 2:00 p.m.
Other residence halls including Hewett, Manchester, Wilkins, Cardinal Court, and Fell/School remain open during break.

SECTION: Winter Break Housing
Watterson, Wright, and Haynie close on December 12 at 2:00 p.m. and reopen January 8, 2027 at 8:00 a.m.
Other halls remain open throughout break.

SECTION: Spring Break Housing
Watterson, Wright, and Haynie close on March 6, 2027 and reopen March 14, 2027 at 2:00 p.m.
Other halls remain open throughout break.
