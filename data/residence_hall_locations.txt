SECTION: Overview of University Housing Locations
Undergraduate housing at Illinois State University is organized into five main residential neighborhoods: Hewett-Manchester, Tri-Towers, Watterson Towers, Cardinal Court, and Fell Avenue and School Street apartments.

All University Housing locations are located within walking distance of campus and provide convenient access to classrooms, academic buildings, athletic facilities, and other campus resources.

Housing options are designed to be both convenient and affordable, while supporting student success and campus engagement.

Parking information and rates are managed separately through the University Parking and Transportation Services website.

SECTION: Residence Hall Experience
All residence halls are located near the University’s main Quad area. Each residential community has its own unique environment and social atmosphere.

Regardless of location, students can expect:
- A sense of community
- Academic and personal support services
- Access to campus resources
- Connection to Illinois State University

SECTION: Hewett-Manchester, Tri-Towers, and Watterson Towers
These traditional residence hall communities include:
- Hewett-Manchester
- Tri-Towers
- Watterson Towers

Each provides a distinct residential experience and is part of the main on-campus housing system.

SECTION: Cardinal Court Apartments
Cardinal Court houses approximately 994 undergraduate students, primarily sophomores.

It offers apartment-style living with:
- Furnished apartments
- Independent living environment
- Continued access to university housing services and benefits

SECTION: Fell Avenue and School Street Apartments
Fell Avenue and School Street apartments provide university-owned off-campus-style housing.

These apartments are available for:
- Upper-class undergraduate students
- Graduate students without dependents
- International students

They offer affordable housing close to campus with university management support.

SECTION: Amenities for All Housing Areas
All residence halls and Cardinal Court include shared amenities such as:
- 24/7 front desk service
- Fitness centers
- Free laundry facilities
- Basketball courts
- Printing services
- Free bus transportation with Redbird ID
- Sand volleyball courts
- Study spaces
- Conference and meeting rooms
- Reflection rooms

SECTION: Off-Campus Housing Support
Students interested in living off-campus can access the university’s off-campus housing database, which lists nearby housing options.

Questions regarding off-campus housing can be directed to the Dean of Students Off-Campus Services at (309) 438-2008.

SECTION: Guest Policy (Minors)
As of August 1, 2025, guests aged 13–17 must have prior parent or guardian approval through a Minor Guest Registration Form.

Guests under 13 years old are only permitted when accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Exceptions may apply during special events such as Homecoming or Siblings Weekends.
Students should refer to the University Housing Services guest policy for full details.
